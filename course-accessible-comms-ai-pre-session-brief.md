### Before the session, please do these four things

**1. Set up two free AI accounts (5 minutes)**

You'll use two AI tools during the session: one to draft your content, one to sense-check it. They need to be from two different providers (different AI tools catch different things, which is part of what makes the cross-check useful).

**Default recommendation**: ChatGPT + Claude
- **ChatGPT**: [https://chat.openai.com](https://chat.openai.com)
- **Claude**: [https://claude.ai](https://claude.ai)

**If you'd prefer a different tool**, any of these combinations work:
- Microsoft Copilot ([copilot.microsoft.com](https://copilot.microsoft.com)) + Claude
- Google Gemini ([gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com)) + Claude
- ChatGPT + Gemini
- Copilot + Gemini

The prompts we'll use in the session work in all four tools. Free tiers are all you need. Test you can log in from the laptop you'll bring on the day.

> **A note on Claude's free tier**: Claude often produces the highest quality drafts for accessible content, but its free tier has tighter daily message limits than ChatGPT, Copilot, or Gemini. For a 2-hour session with lots of iteration, you may hit the cap. That's why we use ChatGPT as the primary drafting tool in the session and Claude as the sense-check (which uses fewer messages).

**2. Log in to Access Compass (2 minutes)**

We've created an Access Compass account for you so you can land directly in the Training Hub on the day.

- **Your login details**: [LOGIN INFO — to be sent in a separate email]
- **Direct course link**: [TRAINING HUB COURSE LINK]

You'll use Access Compass to follow the lessons during the session and to take the prompt pack home. Have a poke around the platform while you're there. You're in the front row of a pilot cohort, so what you see is what other businesses across the country are about to get.

**3. Pre-pick a format (optional, 5 minutes)**

You'll be creating one piece of accessible communication. You can decide in the room, but pre-picking saves time. Most popular for businesses like yours:

- **Accessibility Guide** — info to help customers plan their visit
- **Social Story / Visual Narrative** — first-visit walk-through for autistic and neurodiverse customers
- **Easy Read** — simplified info for readers with intellectual disability or low literacy
- **Plain Language** — anything you want a broad audience to understand quickly

A short description of each is in the course in Access Compass.

**4. Upload source material (optional but useful)**

- 200 to 400 words of any business content you'd like to convert into your chosen format
- Examples: a "what to expect" webpage, an FAQ, a brochure paragraph, a menu, signage copy, a booking confirmation email
- **Upload it to our shared folder in advance** at [SHARED FOLDER LINK] so it's ready on the day, no rummaging. You can also bring it on a USB or in an open browser tab if you'd rather sort it on the day.
- **You don't need a finished document.** Notes, bullet points, photos with captions, or even a website link work fine. If you have nothing prepared, the AI will run you through a checklist of questions and your answers become the source.

We'll be using free AI tools, and free tiers may use your input to train their models. Please don't paste anything confidential into the AI: no customer details, account numbers, internal financials, or staff information. Stick to content that's already public or destined to be public.

## Why each item is in the brief

For your reference, not to send to participants.

| Item | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| Two accounts in advance | If 12 people each spend 5 min creating accounts in the room, that's 60 min lost. Out of a 120 min session. |
| Pre-pick format | Saves 5 min of room time + lets them mentally rehearse before arriving |
| Source material in advance | The biggest time-sink risk on the day. Sourcing or inventing 200 words can eat 20 min if unprepared. |
| Pre-created AC accounts + direct link | Removes friction. Avoids 12 first-timers stumbling through sign-up + Discovery + finding the right page. |
| Cohort context | Builds peer-network expectation, frames the room positively before they walk in. |
| Privacy note | Real risk with free tiers + the businesses you're working with may have sensitive content. Heads off issues. |
| Access needs question | You're running an accessibility course. If the venue isn't accessible for a participant, the credibility hit is high. |
| Stuck-on-setup contact | Saves them showing up cold. Saves you triaging in the first 15 min. |
