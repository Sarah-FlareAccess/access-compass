# Access Compass - Pricing Model (Draft for Review)

**Date:** March 2026
**Status:** Working draft - seeking feedback

---

## What is Access Compass?

A self-guided accessibility assessment platform for businesses and venues. Users complete a tailored discovery process that recommends relevant assessment modules based on their industry and customer touchpoints. The platform generates prioritised reports, action plans, and (optionally) a Disability Inclusion Action Plan (DIAP).

**Two assessment depths:**

- **Pulse Check** - "Where do I stand?" High-level assessment for businesses wanting a starting point. Shows where to focus first.
- **Deep Dive** - "I'm committed, show me everything." Detailed question-by-question assessment for businesses motivated to maximise accessibility and remove specific barriers.

## Individual Module Purchases (Deep Dive Only)

For businesses that want to assess specific areas without committing to a full tier. All individual module purchases are Deep Dive depth (not Pulse Check). Available after completing the free tier.

| Option | Price | Per module |
|---|---|---|
| Single module | $99 | $99 |
| 3-module bundle | $249 | $83 |
| 5-module bundle | $379 | $76 |

**Natural crossover points (by design):**
- At 9 modules ($891 individually), the Starter tier ($799, Pulse Check) is better value
- At 16 modules ($1,584 individually), the Committed tier ($1,500, Deep Dive) is better value
- Discovery typically recommends 10-20 modules, so most businesses land above the crossover

Module spend can be credited toward a tier upgrade (same as tier-to-tier upgrades).

## Open Questions for Discussion

1. **Tier 4 payment terms** - 3 monthly payments at $1,900 is ~$633/mo. Is that too steep for the Pulse Check audience? Should it be 6 monthly?
2. **Tier 6 range** - $6k-$15k is wide. What triggers the higher end? Site count? User count? Support level?
3. **Comparison run pricing** - What should a one-off re-assessment cost as an add-on?
4. **DIAP add-on pricing** - For Tier 5, what should the DIAP add-on cost?
5. **Free tier conversion** - Is 1 free module enough to demonstrate value and drive upgrades?
6. **Contract terms** - Should upgrade credit have an expiry window (e.g. within 12 months)?
