---

## SAM Filters (~250K)

The SAM narrows the TAM to businesses where:
- Access Compass has relevant modules (37 modules across 6 groups)
- State or federal accessibility regulation creates demand (Premises Standards, DDA, state DIAP requirements)
- Reputational or commercial incentive exists (tourism accreditation, council requirements, brand standards)
- Target sectors: hospitality, tourism, attractions, retail, events, health, education, local government

## Key Assumptions and Risks

| Assumption | Risk |
|---|---|
| 800K customer-facing businesses | ABS data shows ~2.5M total. ~32% customer-facing with physical/event presence is conservative |
| $1,200 blended average | Sensitive to tier mix. If free tier converts poorly and most buyers stay at Starter, average drops to ~$800 |
| 500-1,000 in 3 years | Dependent on council partnerships materialising. Without them, SOM is closer to 150-300 |
| Regulatory tailwind | Premises Standards 2024 amendment and state DIAP requirements are driving demand. If enforcement stays weak, urgency drops |
