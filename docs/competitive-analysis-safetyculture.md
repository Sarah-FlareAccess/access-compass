# SafetyCulture Competitive Analysis (Mar 2026)

## Company Overview
- **Formerly**: iAuditor
- **Users**: 85,000+ organisations
- **Positioning**: "Workplace Operations Platform" / "Improvement Operating System"
- **Tagline**: "A better way of working"
- **Target**: Frontline teams and their leaders

## Pricing
| Tier | Price | Details |
|------|-------|---------|
| Free | $0 | Up to 10 users, 5 active templates, basic analytics/training/tasks |
| Premium | $24/seat/month (annual) | Unlimited templates, advanced analytics, integrations, SSO |

## Key Features (14 Modules)
1. Inspections, Audits, Checklists (10,000+ templates)
2. Training and Upskilling (bite-sized, create in minutes)
3. Assets (digital register, maintenance scheduling)
4. Sensors and IoT (real-time monitoring)
5. Documents (centralised, AI-powered answers)
6. Issue and Incident Reporting (app or QR code)
7. Investigations (root cause analysis)
8. Heads Up Communications (internal messaging)
9. Tasks and Actions (from inspections or ad-hoc)
10. Analytics (real-time dashboards, KPIs)
11. Contractor Management (prequalification, compliance)
12. Lone Worker (safety monitoring)
13. Integrations (API, builder, partner network)
14. Marketplace (safety equipment ordering, AU/US only)

Manufacturing, Construction, Hospitality, Retail, Transport/Logistics, Healthcare, Facilities Management, Mining

## Key Differentiator vs Access Compass
SafetyCulture is broad operational compliance (safety, quality, operations) with zero accessibility/disability-specific features. Access Compass owns the accessibility niche:
- Accessibility-specific knowledge in every question
- DIAP generation (Australian legal compliance)
- Standards mapping (AS 1428.1, Premises Standards)
- Resource Hub with accessibility guidance
- Discovery engine tailored to venue type

## Inspiration for Access Compass

### Adopt
- Outcome-led messaging with real stats on landing page
- Social proof section (customer logos, testimonials, ratings)
- Clean hero with platform screenshots
- Mobile-first messaging (PWA capability)

### Consider

## Outcome-Led Messaging: Recommended Stats

### Verified / High Confidence
- "Disability discrimination is the #1 complaint category in Australia, making up over 40% of all human rights complaints." (Source: AHRC Annual Reports)
- "4.4 million Australians live with disability. That is 1 in 5 of your potential customers." (Source: ABS Survey of Disability, Ageing and Carers 2018)
- "An estimated $12.4 billion is spent annually in accessible tourism in Australia." (Source: Travability / Tourism Research Australia)
- "88% of consumers with disabilities say they would return to a business that provides good accessibility." (Source: Open Doors Organization, US)

### Use with Softened Wording
- "Disability discrimination complaints can cost businesses $50,000 or more in legal and settlement costs."
- "Research suggests the majority of people with disabilities have walked away from a business due to accessibility barriers."

### Drop (Unverifiable)
- "Only 3% of businesses meet basic accessibility standards" (misapplied WebAIM web-only stat)
