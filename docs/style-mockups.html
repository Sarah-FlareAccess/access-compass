Quick Comparison

Modern Corporate

  • Best for: Enterprise, Government
  • Tone: Professional, Authoritative
  • Colors: Navy, Sky Blue, Slate
  • Typography: Inter (sans-serif)
  • Key trait: Data-driven, structured

Warm & Welcoming

  • Best for: Tourism, Community
  • Tone: Friendly, Supportive
  • Colors: Navy, Orange, Teal
  • Typography: Nunito (rounded)
  • Key trait: Human, encouraging

Bold & Accessible

  • Best for: Modern brands, Advocacy
  • Tone: Confident, Dynamic
  • Colors: Dark navy, Mint, Pink
  • Typography: Space Grotesk (bold)
  • Key trait: High contrast, energetic
