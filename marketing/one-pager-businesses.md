---

## Why It Matters

- **5.5 million Australians** live with disability. That is 1 in 5 of your customers.
- **$29.2 billion** is spent annually by accessible tourism visitors alone (Tourism Research Australia, 2024)
- **75% of people with disability** have walked away from a business due to poor access, and they take an average of 3.4 people with them
- Under the **Disability Discrimination Act 1992**, all Australian businesses have a legal obligation to provide accessible goods, services, and facilities
- AHRC complaint settlements range from **$10,000 to $100,000+**

Accessibility is not just the right thing to do. It is a legal requirement and a business opportunity.

## How It Works

1. **Tell us about your business** (2 minutes): industry, size, what your customers experience
2. **Complete the assessment** (20-40 minutes): guided questions with help content explaining what each one means
3. **Get your report**: strengths, gaps, and prioritised actions
4. **Use the Resource Hub**: practical solutions from quick wins to comprehensive upgrades, with standards references
5. **Build your action plan**: auto-generated DIAP with assigned actions, due dates, and progress tracking
6. **Improve and reassess**: track progress over time
